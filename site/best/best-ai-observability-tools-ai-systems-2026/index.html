Developing, deploying, and maintaining AI systems introduces unique challenges that traditional observability solutions often struggle to address. From monitoring model drift and prompt engineering effectiveness to tracking GPU utilization and LLM token usage, the complexity of AI workloads demands specialized tools. This guide is for developers and DevOps engineers looking to implement robust monitoring for their AI systems. We'll review leading tools, both dedicated observability platforms and AI-adjacent developer tools, to help you understand their strengths, weaknesses, and how they fit into a modern AI development and operations workflow.

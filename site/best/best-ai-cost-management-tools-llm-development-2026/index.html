From API consumption and inference expenses to the infrastructure required for fine-tuning and deployment, costs can escalate rapidly if not actively monitored and optimized. This guide is for developers, DevOps engineers, and tech leads looking to implement robust AI cost management strategies for their LLM projects. We'll explore a range of tools that directly address cloud spend, enhance developer productivity to reduce labor costs, and streamline workflows to prevent budget overruns.
